Guest Lectures

Dr. Ravi K Ahuja(Founder and CEO,Optym)

An academician who transitioned into entrepreneurship, established Optym in 2000 to pioneer advanced optimization methods in transportation and logistics. An alumnus of IIT Kanpur, under his leadership Optym expanded to include nearly 300 skilled experts across four nations. In 2020, he successfully divested the airline division to Amadeus while persistently innovating decision automation and optimization solutions for railroads, airlines, trucking, and mining firms.

Prof. M K Tiwari(Director, IIM Mumbai)

An experienced professor with a track record in the Manufacturing and Logistics sector. Proficient in Mathematical Modeling, Supply Chain Optimization, and crafting Random Search algorithms for manufacturing and logistics. Possesses expertise in Executive Coaching and research-based teaching in Manufacturing Operations. Formerly served as a professor at IIT Kharagpur.

Dr. Amit Kumar Das(Head of Operations Technology, Air India Digital and Tech)

Dr. Amit Kumar Das, the Head of Operations Technology at Air India Digital & Tech, brings over 23 years of extensive experience in the airline IT domain. His expertise encompasses various IT modules and applications, including Passenger Service System, Revenue Management, Cargo Automation, Flight Operations, and more. Currently, he's focused on AI Enterprise Architecture, Management Reports Automation, and IT system integration, demonstrating his prowess in analytics, enterprise architecture, and airline operations technology.

Mano_Kanthanathan(Managing Partner, FlexSim)

Mano Kanthanathan, Managing Partner at FlexiSim, boasts over 8 years of experience in business development, consulting, and training in enterprise simulation, optimization, and 3D geometric animation across various industries, including coal mines, steel plants, manufacturing, supply chain networks, and warehousing. With expertise in supply chain consulting, and data analytics, and over 20 years of experience in the US in supply chain functions, Mano brings a wealth of knowledge to the table..

Goda Ramkumar(Behind the Scenes of Customer Journey at Swiggy: AI and Optimization)

The topic delves into the inner workings of Swiggy, to uncover how they employ cutting-edge Artificial Intelligence (AI) and optimization techniques to improve the overall customer experience. Ms Goda Ramkumar is a distinguished expert in the field of data science, making her insights highly valuable. During her talk, Ms Ramkumar will shed light on several critical aspects of Swiggy's operations: Delivery Optimization, ETA Prediction, and Demand Forecasting.

Bala Srinivasan(Modern Airline Retailing)

The topic, 'Modern Airline Retailing', revolves around the contemporary strategies and technologies that airlines employ to sell their services and products effectively. It encompasses various aspects of the airline industry, including pricing, ancillary services, customer experience, and distribution channels. With over 12 years of experience in airline revenue management and operations research, Mr Srinivasan is well-equipped to provide valuable insights into this dynamic field.

Deeptak Chatterjee(Industrial Automation: IoT and Digital Twin)

Mr. Deeptak's notable accomplishments encompass over 15 years of expertise in the field of Plant Design and Engineering, specifically focusing on Electrical, Instrumentation, and Automation within the Steel Plant industry. He has been actively involved in prominent Steel Industry ventures and possesses a high level of proficiency in 3D Engineering, Virtual Construction, PLM (Product Lifecycle Management) applications, as well as the development of L1 applications.

Madhava Venkatesh(Blockchain 101)

Join us for a journey through 'Blockchain 101', a talk by Mr. Madhava Venkatesh R, Co-Founder and CTO of TrusTrace, with 17+ years of tech experience. Explore blockchain, a technology changing the game. Mr. Madhava Venkatesh R will introduce core ideas: decentralization and immutability. See how blockchain transforms finance, supply chains, and healthcare. Discover how it enhances security and transparency. We'll showcase real-world examples of how blockchain, AI, and IoT work together. Don't miss this chance to learn from an industry expert!
